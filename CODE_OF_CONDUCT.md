# Code of Conduct

InsightFlare aims to be a practical, respectful open source project.

## Expected Behavior

Participants are expected to:

- Be respectful and professional
- Discuss ideas and code on their technical merits
- Assume good intent, but accept correction when impact differs from intent
- Keep issues and pull requests focused on the project
- Give and receive feedback constructively

## Unacceptable Behavior

Unacceptable behavior includes:

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks
- Discriminatory language or conduct
- Deliberate disruption of discussions
- Publishing private information without permission
- Repeated off-topic or bad-faith interaction

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments, close issues or pull requests, or restrict participation when behavior is harmful to the project.

To report a conduct issue, email:

```text
contact@insightflare.net
```

Reports should include relevant links, screenshots, and context when possible.

Maintainers will review reports and take action based on the severity and pattern of behavior.
